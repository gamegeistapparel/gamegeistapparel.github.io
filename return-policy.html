Returns and Exchanges

Returns

Due to being a small company and inventory always being low, unless there is damage to the product when received, we do not allow returns. Please check our size guide and/or email us at gamegeistapparel@gmail.com to make sure you are getting the size you need!

Exchanges

Dependent upon availability, you can exchange the same product for a different size. Please email us at gamegeistapparel@gmail.com with the subject line "Exchange Inquiry" to ensure we can properly assist you.
