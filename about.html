About Us

We created GameGeist Apparel with the hopes of bringing about a new spirit experience to both men & women. Geist, pronounced guy • st, is a German word for spirit; to us, being a fan is all about giving your team the spirit and energy to win. Driven by the vocation to make today’s trends and pop culture more accessible to the greater collegiate gameday community, our brand is about keeping up with new styles and trends while providing all college students with an awesome selection of clothing for any gameday event.

What Gameday Means to Us

CEO

"Gamedays for me are the best days of the year. There is nothing like the feeling I get on gamedays — everyone getting together to tailgate and root their team to victory is such a rush of excitement. My friends and I all plan out our outfits because the only thing better than watching your team win is looking good while you do it." — Clarence Diogo.


CTO

Class of 2022: get ready to hear The Killers or Blink-182 play at least a dozen times!" — Ocean Ronquillo-Morgan.
